Welcome to our Animal Research Website!

Have you ever wanted to learn about an animal you really like ore have wanted to find other opinions on animals that you think are really cool, but are just unable to find any opinion pieces? Well, your countless days spent browsing for such content is over, because we here at Favourite Animal Discovery aim to give you opinion pieces on a wide range of animals that you maybe might have not even heard of! For this very task we have a team (consisting of one person) who has dedicated their time into researching various animals in order to help you viewers find like-minded opinions or even to learn new perspectives on creatures from all across the animal Kingdom.

How are our Researchers Qualified for the Job?

That’s the best part... we're not qualified by any professional standards for this task that we are taking on! … Wait, don’t leave, hear me out. We may not have any professional/academic titles or certifications that prove that we are capable of providing high-level education on these topics, but out researcher(s) spend hours upon hours watching documentaries, YouTube videos, and just walking outside to observe wildlife around them! It is for this reason that we believe that their expertise (along with the help of Wikipedia) will be more than enough to satisfy your desire to learn more about the animals you love!

What kinds of animals can you expect to see on this list?

You can expect to see animals from many different animal families and species. There will be no shortage of animals to find on this list (hopefully). For example, here are a few places the animals you will see might be from:

  • Canada
  • America
  • Kenya
  • Taiwan
  • Russia
  • Brazil
  • Etc.

As you can see, there are many places that the animals on this website will originate from. We hope that this diverse selection will allow you to be find opinions on animals that you agree with without difficulty.

Is this a Real Website?

Well, the website is real in the sense that it exists, but it is not managed or financed by any large cooperation. This website is maintained entirely by the researcher(s) that provide the opinion pieces. Before you ask (because of course you wanted to ask), we do NOT have any method of allowing our viewers to contribute to our research by donation. Noone who is involved with our website will ask you to donate money or to provide any banking information. If someone does reach out to you asking for such things...well.... just don’t give them money. We have no legal responsibility for any money lost due to scams, and therefore will not be obligated to provide aid in tracking down the criminal. Unless we feel like it. Usually, we don’t feel like it.

How can we contact researchers?

Every opinion article in the blog-brary will have the contact information for the researcher who wrote the piece. If you feel that there is an issue with one of the opinion pieces written by a researcher, you can contact them that way.
